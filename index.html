VAANI SHADIJA

NAIL AMOR : THE NAIL SALON

2020! The year when the pandemic hit the world there was complete lockdown and we didn’t had anything to do apart from online studies and scrolling Instagram. I was in 9th standard when everything was online and one day while scrolling Instagram I saw some nail art pictures. From that day I wanted to learn those art on nails. I just thought why shouldn’t I make this lockdown a little productive and improve my skills on my intrest area so, I just enrolled for a professional nail art course.
After completion and practised regularly and few months later I became “Nail artist” and started my own nail brand by the name “NAIL AMOR” which was launched in 13 july 2020. All this when i was just in 9th standard. At the age of 15 I started earning along with my studies. When I reached 11th standard I chose commerce as my stream which gave me good knowledge about business and helped me implement that in my business as well and made me a business minded girl. In 12th standard while studying “business studies” we had to make a project on the chapter “ MARKETING ” which I can proudly say that I made that project on my very own business “nail amor” I chose nail artist as my profession out of my passion, and will continue to follow my passion along with my studies.

PRETTY PEARLS

Pretty Pearls - 'One for You, One for Me'
Pretty Pearls,as the name suggests deals in beaded jewelry that includes pearls and variety of beads. Pretty Pearls bracelets has a special way of connecting the emotions , when you wear a bracelet that holds sentimental values, it can evoke emotions and memories associated with that person or occasion. It becomes a symbol of friendship,love or even personal strength. You can gift a bracelet in every occasion, whether it be birthday, anniversary or even at farewell of your loved one's.
I use to love art and crafts from childhood, i loved making rings and necklaces from wire and crystals. So,i turned my passion for art into a business and it worked out. I started my business in july 2023 , and I've sold more than 500 bracelets till now. We've a variety of beads to choose from ,for fashion,for good luck ,you name and we make them for you. My mom was my biggest supporter of all time, she used to be up with me late night and help me complete bulk orders in time. My dad helped me with packaging and labeling as he has a printing business. The branding was solely my idea, deciding a brand name& brand mark.
Pretty Pearls gave me the confidence and a glimpse or hope for being independent, it gave me respect, exposure of the business world , opportunity to contribute financially in many ways and overall personality development. I would like to conclude by saying take risks , it's all gonna be worth it at the end. I think this is the new change for new india, this is the time to grow and glow for all.
